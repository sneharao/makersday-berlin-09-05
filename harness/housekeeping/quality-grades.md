# Quality Grades

Assessment of code quality by domain area and architectural layer. Grades are subjective estimates based on codebase exploration.

> **Note:** Specific counts are intentionally omitted — they go stale silently. Re-survey when prioritising improvements or after significant work in an area.

Last updated: 2026-04-07

## Grading Scale

| Grade | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| A | Excellent — well-tested, well-typed, clean patterns |
| B | Good — solid conventions, minor gaps |
| C | Adequate — functional but has noticeable issues |
| D | Below standard — significant gaps or inconsistencies |
| F | Poor — needs major rework |
